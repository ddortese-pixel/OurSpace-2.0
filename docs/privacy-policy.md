# OurSpace 2.0 — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 11, 2026

## Data We Collect
- Email address and display name upon registration
- Profile content you voluntarily share (photos, bio, posts)
- Usage data (login times, feature interactions)
- Device information (OS, app version)

## How We Use Your Data
- To operate and improve the platform
- To send notifications about activity on your profile
- To enforce community guidelines and safety

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We may share data with service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

## End-to-End Encryption
- Direct messages (The Shield) are E2EE — we cannot read them

## Your Rights
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Export your data upon request
